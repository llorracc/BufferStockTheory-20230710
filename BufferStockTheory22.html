19Impatience conditions have ﬁgured in intertemporal optimization problems since the beginning, e.g. in Ramsey (1928). These issues are so central that it would be hopeless to attempt to cite conditions in every other paper that correspond to conditions named and brieﬂy exposited here. I make no claim to novelty for any condition aside from those implicated in my theorems, whose forerunners will be articulated.
